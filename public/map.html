Prioritization Map

The map below shows the data used in the GI and urban greening prioritization process in Bay Point and East Contra Costa county. Applying an earth systems approach to describe the interconnected nature of the urban environment,  we collected data from the four major earth systems, the hydrosphere, geosphere, atmosphere and biosphere. We utilized this information along with community and stakeholder input to help inform new locations for GI in Bay Point. Please explore the data below
